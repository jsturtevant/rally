## Enforcement

The policy prohibits:
- `gh pr create/merge/close/comment`
- `gh issue close/comment/edit`
- `gh api` with POST/PUT/PATCH/DELETE
- `git push`
- MPC tools that mutate external state

This ensures Copilot launched via `rally dispatch` can only analyze and prepare — all mutations require human review.
